Air Jordan is a line of basketball shoes produced by Nike, Inc. Related apparel and accessories are marketed under Jordan Brand. The silhouette of Michael Jordan served as inspiration to create the "Jumpman" logo.

The Air Jordan is famous because these shoes are made with the highest quality materials. There will be a cost connection to any product made with high-quality material. However, people have seen that the cost associated with their Air Jordan is not too much, unlike other shoes of comparable quality.
